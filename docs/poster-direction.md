# Poster Direction

## Recommended Lines

Use only three text layers on the poster:

1. `Scan to explore Telugu stories`
2. `A little book of Telugu wonder`
3. `Stories, wit, poetry, temples, and skyward dreams`

Optional short CTA if the layout needs one:

- `Point your camera here`

Do not add:

- date
- time
- venue
- RSVP
- long URL if the QR is already visible

## QR Layout

Keep the QR as the largest functional element.

Suggested arrangement:

- `point-left.svg` or `point-right.svg`: place near the QR edges as directional cues
- `point-up.svg` or `point-down.svg`: place above or below the QR to frame it
- `person-tipping-hand.svg`: place on one side as the main “presenting” character
- `person-raising-hand.svg` or `waving-hand.svg`: use as a lighter secondary side accent
- `open-book.svg`: small thematic accent near the headline or subtitle
- `rocket.svg`: small lower-corner accent to hint at the Sriharikota chapter
- `sparkles.svg`: use sparingly around the title, not over the QR
